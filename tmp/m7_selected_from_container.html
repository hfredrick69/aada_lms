Which chairside behaviours support four-handed dentistry efficiency?

  • Exchange handpieces across the patient's chest to reduce assistant reach.
  • Skip wedges when using sectional matrices if contacts seem tight.
  • Stabilize the HVE with a palm-thumb grasp and mirror retraction for visibility.
  • Schedule microbreaks every 20 minutes to reset posture and reduce strain.
  • Position the assistant 4-6 inches above the operator with hips level to the patient's shoulder.
